Why Choose loreal Salon?

Weddings are all about creating memories with your loved ones. And picture-perfect memories require you to look picture perfect! Who better than the pioneers of beauty in India to help you look perfect on your big day?! That’s right! For decades Lakmé salon has been helping Indian brides in the country look their absolute best on the most important day of their life.When it comes to bridal makeup, Lakmé Salon believes that every bride must feel like a Queen on their big day. The experts at Lakmé Salon have specially designed and curated looks for keeping in mind the diverse brides of our country. Be it the engagement, mehendi, haldi, cocktail, sangeet or the main wedding, the experts at Lakmé Salon are here to ensure you put your best face forward and create those picture-perfect memories.

The Bridal Team

“At loreal Salon, we work doubly hard than our peers to make sure the bride feels like a QUEEN and can create picture perfect memories of a lifetime. We understand that every bride is unique and our aim is to enhance her beauty while making her look the best version of herself. From the engagement and pre-wedding events to the actual wedding day, we accompany you on every step of the way as you embark on this important journey. Our experts go through multiple rounds of training to bring to you best-in-class and latest trends from the world of beauty.”
– Anupma Katyal, National Creative Director, Makeup Loreal Salon.

When you book a bridal appointment at Lakmé Salon, be rest assured you will be in the hands of highly trained professionals. Each expert at Lakmé Salon undergoes vigorous training and upskilling to stay in the know of current trends.
